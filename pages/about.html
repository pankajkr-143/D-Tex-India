Our Story

D TEX INDIA, based in Surat – India's textile hub, manufactures and supplies premium yarns and textile solutions. We combine innovation, quality and reliability to serve domestic and global markets with quick turnaround.

Our vision is to weave excellence, sustainability and customer trust in every strand—across nylon twisted yarn, DTY, metallic yarns, fabrics and weaving accessories.

Founded with a passion for quality and innovation, we have grown from a small family business to a trusted name in the textile industry. Our commitment to excellence has made us a preferred partner for leading brands worldwide.
